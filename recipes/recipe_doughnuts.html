Fried donuts, like grandma's

Description of the dish

Such pies are prepared without adding kefir, but with yeast - the cooking technology is simple, and the dish turns out to be very tasty.

Ingredients

For the dough

  • Wheat flour - 3 cups;
  • Fast-acting dry yeast - 7 g;
  • Sugar - 1 tbsp. l;
  • Salt - 1 tsp;
  • Water - 300 ml;
  • Vegetable oil - 50 gr.

For the filling

  • potatoes - 500 g;
  • onion - 1 pc.;
  • salt, pepper - to taste.

Recipe instructions

  1. Mix yeast, sugar, salt, warm water and 5 tablespoons of sifted flour in a cup. Mix, cover with a lid or a plate, leave in a dark, dry place for 20-30 minutes.
  2. After that, pour vegetable oil and pour the remaining sifted flour. Stir the dough with a spoon, and then knead it thoroughly with your hands. Grease a deep container with oil, put the dough in it, cover with a bag and put it in a warm place for 1 hour.
  3. Peel, boil, salt and mash the potatoes. Peel the onion, cut into cubes, fry in oil until cooked, add to the puree and mix. Sprinkle the table with flour, take out the dough, tear off several pieces from it. Form balls from them, leave them for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Then make cakes from the balls, put the filling on each one and pinch the edges of the dough. Pour oil into the pan, lay out the dough, fry the patties on both sides until golden.
